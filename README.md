# Toman-Bike-Shop-Analysis
A beginner's data analysis project using SQL Server and Power BI

**Description**
SQL was used for data cleaning and calculation of main KPIs that directly influence business decision making process:
- Ridership
- Profit
- Revenue
- Monthly Difference in Revenue
- Net Profit Margin
- 3-Day Moving Average

Power BI was used for data visualization to better explain business insights from data to stakeholders:
- Does the increase in price from one year to another affects ridership and financial performance?
- Is there a correlation between environmental factors and monthly ridership ?
- Revenue usually drops in what month and why ?
- What month saw the highest ridership and why ?
