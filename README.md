# Toman-Bike-Shop-Analysis
A beginner's data analysis project using SQL Server and Power BI

**Description**

_SQL_ was used for data cleaning and calculation of main KPIs that directly influence business decision making process:
- Ridership
- Profit
- Revenue
- Monthly Difference in Revenue
- Net Profit Margin
- 3-Day Moving Average

_Power BI_ was used for data visualization to better explain business insights from data to stakeholders:
- Does the increase in price from one year to another affects ridership and financial performance?
- Is there a correlation between environmental factors and monthly ridership ?
- Revenue usually drops in what month and why ?
- What month saw the highest ridership and why ?

**Key Insights**

1. Despite the 25% increase year-over-year in price, ridership still maintained substantial growth of 64%
Therefore, it is quite safe to say that if we increase the price to about 10 - 15%, it should not affect ridership (price elasticity of + 2.56),

2. A correlation between ridership and environmental factors was examined. Most riders were found on months that have low to medium humidity.
Interestingly, there were still a considerable amount of riders that accept higher rate of humidity as long as windspeed is ideal.

3. Revenue have grown steadily and reached its peak in April 2021 and March 20222. However, months between October and December of both years have witnessed drops in revenue, this is perhaps because of the environmental factors that I have mentioned above.

**Recommendations**

1. **Market Analysis:** Further market analysis should be conducted to understand customers needs as well as their satisfaction, potential competitive changes
and overall economic environment. This can serve as a guide to determine the price later on.

2. **Change in strategy:** Evaluate main and alternative strategies to tackle revenue drops in later months of the year and find out ways on how to attract customers in earlier months of the year where the weather is ideal and make sure to properly advised customers who want to use our service in months.
with bad weather.
